Current HR Jobs

As a member of the Senior Leadership Team (SLT) you would be expected to be office based 5 days a week (post-pandemic). You will have one direct report, at least initially. The HR function is recently established, but it is quite immature and there is plenty of areas you can make an impact and shape the function; so would suit someone who maybe has worked in smaller organisations or a start-up environment or simply enjoys the mixture of strategic input and execution. If you like a broad remit and variety in an ambitious company, then this could be perfect for you.

Responsibilities include:

  • Create and drive the HR Strategy linked to the business plan.
  • Driving organisational effectiveness
  • Talent Management
  • Reward
  • Recruitment and Retention
  • Employee Relations
  • Line management of one other HR team member

Person specification

  • Previous experience of working at strategic and operational HR levels.
  • Previous line management experience is essential.
  • Financial services experience is not necessary, but it would be an advantage to have worked in a regulated industry previously.
  • ‘hands on’ approach
  • Ability to influence and build rapport with employee base.
  • Happy to be office based – 5 days per week post pandemic

Key Deliverables:

  • Full ownership of the resourcing strategy which is not currently fully defined.
  • Providing best practice guidance and support to operational teams including expertise in relation to innovative resourcing techniques and attraction methods.
  • Employer branding support and champion of a fantastic candidate experience
  • Working with and engaging external recruitment partners to support on searches and filling roles directly where possible.
  • Hire recruitment team members and line manage your newly established team.

Person specification

  • Previous experience of working inhouse/RPO recruitment and providing resourcing/recruitment strategic input and operational expertise.
  • Exceptional communication skills and ability to build relationships with stakeholders and candidates.
  • Proactive approach to your role and finding good solutions for stakeholders.
  • An understanding of the IT/tech sector and the breadth of tech roles would be a distinct advantage.
