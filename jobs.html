Current HR Jobs

Key responsibilities include:

  • Working with the HR team to deliver our talent acquisition strategy.
  • Finding creative ways to attract the best talent on the market.
  • Working closely with line managers to support them through the recruitment and selection process and assist them in making high quality decisions.
  • Partnering with suppliers to ensure market trends and insights are used to help drive results.
  • Manage and deliver a positive the candidate experience.
  • Working with the wider team to develop their career site, create social media content where required; ensure their brand is consistently conveyed throughout the candidate experience/process.

Person Specification:

  • A proven track record in recruitment whether inhouse, RPO or Agency (or a combination)
  • Previous experience and knowledge in attracting and sourcing within the IT/Tech sector
  • Some knowledge of recruiting internationally would be an advantage, but not essential.
  • Strong stakeholder management skills
  • Organised and be able to prioritise your work
  • Team player and proactive approach to your work

As a member of the Senior Leadership Team (SLT) you would be expected to be office based 5 days a week (post-pandemic). You will have one direct report, at least initially. The HR function is recently established, but it is quite immature and there is plenty of areas you can make an impact and shape the function; so would suit someone who maybe has worked in smaller organisations or a start-up environment or simply enjoys the mixture of strategic input and execution. If you like a broad remit and variety in an ambitious company, then this could be perfect for you.

Responsibilities include:

  • Create and drive the HR Strategy linked to the business plan.
  • Driving organisational effectiveness
  • Talent Management
  • Reward
  • Recruitment and Retention
  • Employee Relations
  • Line management of one other HR team member

Person specification

  • Previous experience of working at strategic and operational HR levels.
  • Previous line management experience is essential.
  • Financial services experience is not necessary, but it would be an advantage to have worked in a regulated industry previously.
  • ‘hands on’ approach
  • Ability to influence and build rapport with employee base.
  • Happy to be office based – 5 days per week post pandemic
