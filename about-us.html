About Us

Founder

HR Recruitment Solutions was set up to be more than a recruitment company. Its founder, Leon Morley is different to the ‘average’ recruiter. Leon progressed in his recruitment career by focusing on pursuing long-term relationships as a trusted partner.

Why?

In establishing HR Recruitment Solutions Leon aims to add value to the customer and the HR profession beyond a transactional recruitment process whilst striving to be the best at the fundamentals.

By adding value beyond the norm and positioning themselves as a key strategic recruitment partner, HR Recruitment Solutions are uniquely positioned to attract talent beyond the results from a recruitment agency or advertisement.
